Simple Process

How It Works

01

Report or Search

Found something around SBHS? Report it with a photo. Lost something? Browse our listings or use search.

02

Admin Review

SBHS staff reviews submissions to ensure accuracy and prevent misuse of the system.

03

Submit Claim

Found your item? Submit a claim with details proving ownership. We'll verify and connect you.

04

Reunited!

Once verified, pick up your item from the SBHS main office. Happy reunion, Viking!

About Viking Vault

Our Mission

Helping the South Brunswick High School community reunite with lost belongings.

Our Purpose

Viking Vault was created to help students and staff at South Brunswick High School quickly and easily reunite with their lost belongings. We believe that every lost item deserves to find its way home.

Fast & Easy

With just a few clicks, you can report a found item or search for something you've lost. Our streamlined process makes it simple for everyone to participate.

Safe & Secure

All submissions are reviewed by SBHS staff before being made public. We verify claims to ensure items are returned to their rightful owners.

Our Values

Honesty

We trust our community to report items accurately and claim only what belongs to them.

Kindness

Helping others find their lost items is an act of kindness that strengthens our community.

Efficiency

We've designed Viking Vault to be fast and easy to use, so items can be reunited quickly.

Community

Together, we can help every Viking find what they've lost and keep our school organized.
